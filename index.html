A Journey of Scent, Serenity, and Success

In a world where artificiality has become the norm, we choose a different path—one that leads back to nature, where purity, authenticity, and wellness thrive. Our journey began with a simple yet profound belief: the best solutions for well-being have always been found in nature. At Aromatik Jaya, we are more than just an essential oil company. We are nature’s storytellers, capturing the soul of botanicals in their purest form. From the rich forests to sun-kissed fields, we carefully source our ingredients from the most pristine environments, working with farmers and artisans who share our passion for sustainability and quality.

Our Products

Azfara Aroma – Premium Essential Oil Products

Azfara Aroma offers a wide range of high-quality essential oils extracted from Indonesia's finest plants. Our products are widely used in the cosmetics, aromatherapy, pharmaceuticals, and food & beverage industries. We ensure that every product meets international standards for purity and quality.

  • Patchouli Oil – Available in Dark, Light, Low Iron variants with PA concentrations of 40-65%
  • Clove Oil – Includes Clove Bud Oil, Clove Leaf Oil, Clove Stem Oil, and Eugenol up to 99.5%
  • Citronella Oil – Available in Java Citronella, Citronellal, Rhodinol, and Geraniol variants
  • Cajeput Oil – With cineole content of a minimum of 55–60%
  • Gurjun Balsam Oil – Perfect as a fixative in perfumes with a woody aroma
  • Alpinia Malaccensis Oil – Also known as wild galangal oil, used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine
